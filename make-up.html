The importance of make-up in women

Make-Up Makes You Look Better, This is the most obvious reasons why a lot of people find make-up important.

Make-up can do a lot of wonderful things to our physical appearance. It can hide unwanted marks on a persons face, improve the skin tone and highlight ones facial features.

We use makeup to increase the external beauty of a person.

How to Apply make-up

Remove any old makeup. When you are applying makeup, it is necessary to start with a clean palette.

Remove any makeup that you might have slept in, and wash off makeup from earlier on in the day.

If you try to apply more makeup over the top of old makeup your end look will appear caked on and more unnatural than makeup put on a fresh face.

You also need to cleanse any makeup residue to keep your skin from getting clogged and irritated.
